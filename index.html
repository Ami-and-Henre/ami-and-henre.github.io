Venue

The wedding will be held at the van Zyl family farm Bloubokkiesfontein in Napier, Western Cape, on Saturday 29 October 2016. The map below shows the exact coordinates, and below it you will find detailed directions. You can also click the button on the map labeled "Directions" to view directions to Napier from anywhere.

The map should be here. If you can't see it, you may need to view this site in a different browser or on a different device.

Directions to No. 7, Aanmekaar Farm, Bloubokkiesfontein, Napier:

  1. When entering Napier on the main road from Caledon's side, turn right onto the road to Elim at the first four-way stop.
  2. Continue along the road to Elim, and just after the tar road ends, you will pass a turnoff to the left - a farm-gate marked Blomfontein. Pass this gate. About a kilometer further, there is a turnoff on the left named Bloubokkiesfontein. Keep going straight.
  3. Just under two kilometers out of Napier, turn left at the wooden sign that reads "Palmietkloof". After taking that turnoff, no. 7 is the second homestead on the left. The farmhouse has a blue roof.
  4. If by chance you miss the Palmietkloof sign, the next turnoff sign to the left is "Magdala". That tells you you have to do a U-turn back to the Palmietkloof turnoff.
