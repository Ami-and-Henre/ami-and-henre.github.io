Menu

Vegetarian, vegan, & gluten-free options will be available.

Starters

  • Roasted veg & halloumi skewer with tzatziki
  • Mini grilled chicken skewers with coconut & lime sauce
  • Beef burger sliders with cheese & caramelised onion

Main

  • Flatbread pockets or salad, with choice of filling:
    • Beef brisket & caramelised onion
    • Lemon & rosemary chicken with tzatziki
    • Roasted veg & feta
  • Salads
    • Summer salad: mixed lettuce, baby spinach, rosa tomatoes, julienne carrots, avocado, & toasted seeds
    • Roasted baby potato salad: slow-roasted baby potatoes with honey mustard mayo & bacon bits on a bed of baby spinach

Coffee

  • Filter coffee or tea
  • Lemon meringue

Dress code

Wear what makes you feel good! As the ground on the farm is slightly uneven, we recommend flatter shoes or wedges rather than stilettos. The weather may be sunny one moment and cool the next, so please bring something warm, especially if you plan on staying into the evening.
